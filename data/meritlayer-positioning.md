## The Competitive Landing Page

**URL:** `/vs/permitflow`

**Headline:** "PermitFlow is built for contractors. MeritLayer is built for developers."

**Subhead:** "If you're pulling permits for a Boston development project — not managing a fleet of trade contractors — there's a better fit."

**Body points:**
1. **Different buyer, different product.** PermitFlow's own testimonials are HVAC companies, roofing contractors, and national GCs doing hundreds of permits. Their product is optimized for volume filing across commodity permits. Boston development involves ZBA variances, Article 80 review, BPDA entitlements — processes PermitFlow doesn't touch.

2. **You keep control.** PermitFlow takes over your permitting. If something goes wrong — a missed deadline, a rejection you didn't know about — you find out after the fact. MeritLayer puts you in the driver's seat with real-time visibility across every project.

3. **Built for your market.** We built MeritLayer in Boston, for Boston developers. We know the difference between a Short Form and a Long Form at ISD. We know Article 80E vs. 80A. We know the ZBA hears every other Tuesday and your project manager better be ready. PermitFlow knows 7,000 cities at surface level. We know Boston deeply.

4. **Self-serve. No sales call.** Start today. Upload a document, get an instant compliance checklist. Know what you're missing before your architect does.

5. **Priced for developers.** Not enterprise-tier. Not per-permit fees. A flat monthly rate that makes sense for the number of active projects you're actually running.

**CTA:** "Start free — no sales call, no contract, no bull."
